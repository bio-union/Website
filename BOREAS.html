TECHNOLOGY BEHIND

BOREAS™ Smart Cooling Garment is currently the lightest and most seamless high-efficiency wearable cooling solution on the market. 
It is jointly cooperated by Makalot and international leading consumer electronics brands.

Different from the cooling experience created by yarn and fabric in traditional cooling clothes on the market (only 1~2 degrees Celsius (°C) difference from room temperature), the BOREAS™  cooling clothing system directly generates the "cold feeling" on the body.

SMART TEMPERATURE CONTROL SYSTEM
Allows users to control and choose their best comfort by switching the mode and level.

HIGH TECH FABRIC
The fabrics are made of unique superb feeling materials. 
In addition to the cold and soft hand feel, we also added the coating, reflecting the near-infrared (NIR) and blocking ultraviolet rays to enhance the cooling experience.

DEVICE STABILIZATION STRUCTURE DESIGN 
( Patented)
Adopting the patented structure by Makalot, the device is firmly attached to the cervical spine, and the weight of the device is dispersed in the clothing through the clothing structure, eliminating the discomfort of the neck choking.

HIGH THERMAL CONDUCTIVE MODULE (Patented)
High-conductivity fabric materials transfer the cold feeling rapidly from the device to the human body and sightly extend the cooling area simultaneously.

FIREFIGHTER and MILITARY

Research shows, humans have indicated that lower temperatures improve judgment ability and performance by as much as 10%~20%. 

SPORTS INJURY

Say good bye to ice bag.
Boreas™ can be an effective modality to limit the extent of your injury as well as to decrease pain and swelling. 
Be careful when using ice shortly before exercise as it may decrease your balance, proprioception, and muscle strength.


People with poor circulation or who suffer from conditions that compromise their circulation should not use our device or ice bag as a treatment without consulting their doctor.
